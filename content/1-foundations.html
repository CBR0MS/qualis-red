FOUNDATIONS

We begin where we are;

at a momentary confluence of shadows under an unseen canopy of boughs.

This is, by my estimation,

something ephemeral and concrete.

Something rooted and adrift.

Something known and unkown.

The pure truth and an absolute lie.

Put simply,

The basis of everything is unknowable.

It could be something, it could be nothing.

To contend with being unable to understand, we create shadows.

Shadows act as the basis of everything.

When compared to the possibility of knowing we fall short.

So we are all retarded.

It is probably more interesting this way.
